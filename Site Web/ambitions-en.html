My Passions

I have multiple passions I love old video-games, I love music, I love electronics, tinker, collect old computers (in addition it's ecological), fabricate, create. But I have preferences in my passions.
My number one passion, it's music. I always loved listening to it, but also to create it. Make a electronic music producer professional career is my childhood dream, and I intend to get there.
However, to be able to do well, I need good equipement, and it cost a lot of money. And so I need a stable job before realizing this dream. And that's where my studies comes in. I'm keen on old video games, I'd like to create some on my own.
However it takes knowledge which are very hard to have by self-education (and I know whet I'm talking about, I tried to code alone, and it didn't work very well).
So at high school, i took the IT speciality, to introduce me to coding, and I decided to continue my studies at Saint-Dié in IT.
Work in the video-games, or IT in general, it's also this the thing I want. I don't know yet if I will continue after. It will be done according to my motivation, according to my desire.

But place to the question of the future.


How do I see myself in 10, 20 or even 30 years ?

For 10 years, I think that the first 5 years will be dedicated in search of money for my dreams, and eventually, after 10 years, work in the video game industry.
Once this done, I will try to commit myself in my dreams career. 5-7 years of transition, where I mix my video-game job, and the music in solo.
Now it's just a supposition. In the worst case, after 7 years, I am caught. I'll have to wait if what I'm doing works..
I think I will need 3 or 5 years more where here, I will be in the 2nd transition phase.
I earn money with my video-game job, and with my music producer career.
Once these 3-5 years done, where I can make music my main job, I resign in my video-game job, and I'm going deep into music. Until the end of everything.

Here is what I think of my future, all is only suppositions, hypotheses, because I don't know if everything will go as I think.
